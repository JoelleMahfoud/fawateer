Privacy Policy

🔒 Your Data Stays on Your Device

Fawateer does not collect, store, or share your personal data. All your invoices, sales, and product information remain entirely on your device unless you choose to export or share them yourself.

📊 Analytics & Advertising

When you use Fawateer, ads may be displayed through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers in accordance with Google's privacy policy. Users who subscribe to remove ads will not see any advertising content.

💳 In-App Purchases

Payment processing for in-app purchases is handled entirely and securely by Apple. Fawateer does not access, store, or process any payment information.

🤝 Third-Party Services

We do not sell or share your data with any third parties. We do not use analytics services, crash reporters, or external databases that store personally identifiable information.
